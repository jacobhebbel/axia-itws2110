Axia Finance Friend

Displays relevant statistics for an investors portfolio and uses mean-variance optimization to calculate and plot the efficient frontier.

Streamlines the process of evaluating your portfolio by displaying all relevant statistics and the efficient frontier in an easy to use, visually appealing manner.

Stay Green

Shows the nearest greenspace in your community, giving back to the environment and promoting interactions with nature.

In a world where reducing your carbon footprint is essential to creating a better tomorrow, we created a resource to encourage others to embrace the beauty of nature.

Interactive Color Pallet Generator

Generates a color pallet based on user preferences, such as themes, colors, mood, tone, and reference hues.

User has the option to generate a color pallet randomly or using their preferences, alleviating the stress of choosing visually appealing color tones for a designers project.
